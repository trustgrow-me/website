Data protection guidelines

TrustGrow provides online reputation, visibility, and brand protection services for companies worldwide. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparent handling of personal data across all our platforms and services.

TrustGrow privacy policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TrustGrow collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you visit or interact with https://trustgrow.me (the "Site").

Personal information we collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect technical information about your browser, IP address, device type, time zone, and cookies installed on your device. We also track which pages you view, how you navigate the Site, and what sources referred you to us. We refer to this as "Device Information."

  • Cookies – Anonymous data files stored on your device. (Learn more: http://www.allaboutcookies.org )
  • Log Files – Track on-site actions including IP address, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps.
  • Web Beacons & Pixels – Used to measure engagement and improve user experience.
  • Analytics – Tools such as Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior and traffic patterns.

When you contact us, request an audit, or submit a form through the Site, we may collect information such as your name, email address, company details, website URL, and any information you voluntarily provide to describe your request.

How we use your personal information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain TrustGrow services
  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • Screen for risk or potential fraud
  • Improve performance and user experience across our Site
  • Deliver service-related updates or promotional materials when permitted

Sharing your personal information

We only share your information with trusted third-party tools essential for operating our services:

  • Analytics tools to understand traffic and performance
  • Email and CRM services to manage communication
  • Secure payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) when applicable

Do not track

The Site does not change its data collection behavior if a browser sends a “Do Not Track” request.

Your rights

If you are located within the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • You may request access, updates, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Contact: legal@trustgrow.me

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for operational and compliance purposes unless you request removal.

Minors

The Site is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

Changes

TrustGrow may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes.
