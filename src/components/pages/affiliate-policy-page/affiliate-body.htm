How does it work?

As a TrustGrow affiliate, you contribute directly to the growth of our client network.
Every qualified referral that becomes a paying customer generates a commission for you. There is no cap — performance determines your upside.

What commission do I earn?

TrustGrow generally offers an affiliate commission of around 10%.
However, final rates vary per case based on volume, deal size, and the type of client you refer. Your dedicated account manager will confirm the exact percentage for your partnership structure.

How will I know if I made a sale?

Each time a referred customer converts, you’ll receive an automatic confirmation. You can also track real-time performance, conversions, and pending payouts inside your affiliate dashboard.

How do I get paid?

  • Payouts are processed through PayPal or another approved method arranged with your account manager.
  • Ensure your payment details are correctly added in your affiliate profile.

When are payouts made?

  • Payouts occur at the end of each month.
  • Minimum release threshold: $10.
  • All commissions undergo manual verification to ensure validity.
  • Example: Earnings generated in May are paid by June 30.

Cookie duration

When a visitor clicks your affiliate link, a 30-day tracking cookie is activated. If the user purchases within this period, the referral is attributed to you — unless a later affiliate link overrides it.
